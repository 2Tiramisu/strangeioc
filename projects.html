Demons with Shotguns

Demons with Shotguns is a fast paced 2D arena platformer where you battle for the souls of your friends. Armed with a powerful shotgun, bullet deflecting shield and explosive holy water, 2-4 players will battle it out in multiple game modes across multiple environments, each featuring unique hazards and mechanics.
